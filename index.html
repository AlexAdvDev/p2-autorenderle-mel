About Autorenderle


A game for Portal 2 speedrunners (and enjoyers) made by Jaio.


An autorender of a top 40 run is randomly selected, and the goal is to figure out who the runner is.


After making your guess, you can see statistics such as time and streak.


Autorenderle is inspired by Rankdle.

Game Filters

Filters are used to select which runs are shown in-game. Do note that your streak will be reset when changing filter settings.
